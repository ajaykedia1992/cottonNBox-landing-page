About CottonNBox
CottonNBox is an ultra-premium ethnic Indian Kurti brand rooted in the rich textile heritage of India. We create 100% pure cotton kurtis that blend centuries-old artisan craftsmanship — including hand block printing, natural dyeing, and embroidery — with contemporary luxury design. Every piece is crafted to be breathable, beautiful, and built to last.
We are currently in our pre-launch phase, with our grand launch countdown live on the website. The exact launch date is displayed on our homepage. Waitlist members will receive exclusive early-access notifications before the public launch.
CottonNBox is proudly based in India. Our cotton is sourced from the finest cotton-growing regions of Gujarat, Maharashtra, and Telangana, and our kurtis are crafted by skilled artisans in traditional textile hubs across Rajasthan, Gujarat, and Andhra Pradesh.
CottonNBox is built on three pillars: purity, heritage, and luxury. We use only 100% pure cotton — no blends, no synthetics. Every garment is crafted using traditional artisan techniques passed down through generations. And we bring this heritage into a luxury fashion context, with editorial-quality design, premium packaging, and a rigorous 12-point quality check on every piece.
Product & Fabric

Our collection features a curated selection of premium Indian cotton fabrics, including fine muslin (mulmul), cambric, voile, and handloom cotton. Each fabric is chosen for its breathability, softness, and suitability for the specific design — ensuring the kurti feels as beautiful as it looks.
Yes. We use only azo-free, skin-safe dyes and natural block printing inks wherever possible. Our printing process uses traditional wooden blocks carved by master craftsmen, and we avoid harsh chemical treatments that can irritate the skin. Every fabric is washed and tested before stitching to ensure it is safe for all skin types, including sensitive skin.
CottonNBox is committed to inclusive sizing. Our kurtis will be available in sizes XS to 3XL. Detailed size charts with measurements in both inches and centimetres will be published at launch. If you have specific sizing concerns, please reach out to us at cottonnbox@gmail.com and we will assist you personally.
Pure cotton kurtis are easy to care for. We recommend: (1) Machine wash on a gentle/delicate cycle in cold water, or hand wash. (2) Use a mild detergent — avoid bleach. (3) Do not tumble dry; instead, dry flat in shade to preserve colour and shape. (4) Iron on medium heat while slightly damp for best results. Detailed care instructions will be included on the label of every garment.

Sustainability & Ethics
Sustainability is at the heart of everything we do. We use natural cotton fibres that are biodegradable. We work with artisans who use traditional, low-waste production methods. Our packaging is designed to be minimal and recyclable. And we produce in limited quantities to avoid overproduction — a commitment to quality over quantity.

By choosing CottonNBox, you are directly supporting the livelihoods of traditional Indian artisans.
